Dairy Cold Stores

Designed to meet the unique demands of dairy products, these rooms excel in blast freezing milk, ice cream, butter, curd, and yogurt. Our state-of-the-art technology ensures precise temperature control, preserving the freshness and quality of dairy commodities at every stage. Aeron's dedicated team, well-versed in the nuances of dairy cold storage, takes pride in safeguarding the integrity of your products. From the creamy texture of ice cream to the rich consistency of butter and the tangy goodness of curd and yogurt, we prioritize maintaining the unique characteristics of each dairy item. Aeron stands as your trusted partner, offering excellence in cold storage solutions for the diverse world of dairy, ensuring that your products are not just frozen but preserved with the utmost care and precision.
